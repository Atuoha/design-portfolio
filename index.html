How I Work

My Design Process

A structured, human-centred approach that delivers thoughtful results consistently.

01

Discover & Define

Deep-dive through user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitor analysis before touching any design tool.

02

Ideate & Wireframe

Rapid sketching, IA mapping, and low-fi wireframes — exploring multiple directions before committing.

03

Design & Prototype

High-fidelity Figma designs with interactive prototypes. Every interaction considered, every visual decision earns its place.

04

Test & Validate

Usability testing with real users. I let data and behaviour guide iteration — not assumptions or ego.

05

Deliver & Handoff

Developer-ready specs and thorough documentation. I stay engaged to ensure the design survives contact with code.

06

Measure & Iterate

Post-launch analytics and continuous improvement. Great design evolves with the product and its users.
